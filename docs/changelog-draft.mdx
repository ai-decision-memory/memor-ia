## New

- **Turn chat conversations into saved documents**
  Generate saved technical or user-facing documents from a chat conversation, with follow-up clarification when needed. Documents can be reopened later from the workspace instead of being lost in the chat thread.

## Improved

- **Added temporary chats so you choose which conversations to save**
  You can now start chats without saving them right away, then choose to keep a conversation once it’s worth preserving. Saved chats can also be created with the conversation and title already carried over.
